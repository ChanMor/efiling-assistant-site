Install & Activate

It takes about two minutes. You'll need the installer and the license key from your purchase e-mail.

On a Mac

  1. Open the .dmg and drag eFiling Assistant into Applications.
  2. The first time, macOS may say it "cannot be opened because Apple cannot check it." This is normal for a small app. Right-click (or Control-click) the app icon → Open → Open. You only do this once.
  3. If you don't see "Open", go to System Settings → Privacy & Security, scroll down, and click "Open Anyway".

On Windows

  1. Run the installer (.exe or .msi).
  2. If Windows shows a blue "Windows protected your PC" box, click "More info" → "Run anyway". This is normal for a new app and only happens until it builds reputation.
  3. Finish the installer and open eFiling Assistant from the Start menu.

Activate & sign in

  1. Paste your license key on the activation screen and click Activate. (One time per computer.)
  2. Click Continue with Google and choose the professional Gmail you file from — this must be your e-mail address of record with the court.
  3. The first time, Google may say "Google hasn't verified this app." Click Advanced → "Go to eFiling Assistant", then allow it to send e-mail on your behalf. (The app can only send — it can't read your inbox.)
  4. That's it — you're in. You won't have to sign in again.
If your license is locked to a specific Gmail, sign in with that exact account, or the app will ask you to switch.
