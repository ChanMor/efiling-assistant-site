Why offices trust it

  • Built on the actual guidelines. A.M. No. 10-3-7-SC & the Interim Rule on Electronic Filing (Rule 13-A).
  • Catches "deemed not filed" traps before you send — non-PDF, password-protected, archives, corrupted files, missing CC, wrong sender.
  • Sends from your own e-mail of record — never a stranger's server. Your documents stay on your computer.
  • Keeps your proof — the transmittal lands in your Gmail "Sent," and you can download a record for the case file.
  • Works on Windows and Mac. No subscription, no accounts to manage — a one-time license per office.
