Install & Activate

It takes about two minutes. You'll need the installer and the license key from your purchase e-mail.

On a Mac

  1. Open the .dmg and drag eFiling Assistant into Applications.
  2. Open it once from Applications. macOS will block it and say it's from an unidentified developer — this is normal for a small independent app and is a one-time step.
  3. Go to System Settings → Privacy & Security, scroll to the bottom, and click “Open Anyway”, then confirm. eFiling Assistant opens and stays trusted from then on.
Still blocked, or it says “damaged”? Open the Terminal app and paste this one line, then press Return — it clears Apple's download flag:
xattr -dr com.apple.quarantine "/Applications/eFiling Assistant.app"
Then open the app normally. You only ever do this once.

On Windows

  1. Run the installer (.exe or .msi).
  2. If Windows shows a blue "Windows protected your PC" box, click "More info" → "Run anyway". This is normal for a new app and only happens until it builds reputation.
  3. Finish the installer and open eFiling Assistant from the Start menu.

Sign in

  1. Click Continue with Google and sign in with the Gmail address you bought eFiling Assistant under — this should be the professional e-mail of record you file from. Your access is tied to this address.
  2. The first time, Google may say "Google hasn't verified this app." Click Advanced → "Go to eFiling Assistant", then allow it to send e-mail on your behalf. (The app can only send — it can't read your inbox.)
  3. That's it — you're in. You won't have to sign in again.
Sign in with the exact address you purchased under. Need to use a different e-mail? Just let us know and we'll switch it.
